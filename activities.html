Activities

Leisure Lodge Beach & Golf Resort has a number of daily and evening activities. A typical day at the hotel would be the following:-
After Breakfast
10.00hrs Body stretching – Coral Beach
11.00hrs Water Aerobics – Bahari Pool
12.00hrs Swahili Lessons / Darts / Water Polo
15.00hrs Beach Boccia / Visit to Leisure Rondwe Jalaram Girls School
17.00hrs Beach volleyball / beach walk
The hotel also has various themes and venues for dinner which will be accompanied with either a band / keyboard / DJ. Also cultural shows will be included.
